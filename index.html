About Me

Who am I?

I am Luke Woods! I was born in 2000 and grew up outside Atlanta, Georgia. I got married to the love of my life in July 2022 and currently live in Utah. I love all things technology and I build my own gaming PC's. I love to play soccer and spend quailty time with my loved ones.

What Have I Done?

I was awarded my the honor of Eagle scout by sending over 3,000 books to kids in Africa. I served a 2 year mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. While doing that I created and ran over 15 free English class across 5 states. I am Adobe illustrator certified and have worked in the tech industry for over 6 years.

What I Want to Do!

I am currently enrolled at Brigham Young University and am studying Information Systems. I plan on gradutating from BYU with my Bachlors in IS and will also pursue a Masters in Information Systems here. I want to further the business world through technology and be a valuable asset to the world!
